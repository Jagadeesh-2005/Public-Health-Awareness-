# 🏥 Health Survey & Awareness App

A Flutter-based mobile application developed to collect community health survey data, generate reports, provide health awareness resources, and gather public suggestions for improving healthcare services.

## 📌 Overview

The Health Survey & Awareness App is designed to assist communities in recording health-related information, analyzing survey responses, and spreading health awareness through videos and educational content.

The application enables users to:

* Submit health survey responses
* View stored survey submissions
* Generate health reports and statistics
* Watch awareness videos
* Access health guidance resources
* View awareness reels
* Submit community suggestions and feedback

---

## 🚀 Features

### 📝 Health Survey Form

* Collects participant information
* Stores survey responses locally
* Validates user input before submission

### 📋 Survey Submissions

* Displays all submitted survey records
* Shows participant details and health issues
* Loads saved survey data automatically

### 📊 Reports Dashboard

* Total survey submissions count
* Health issue distribution
* Age-group analysis
* Community health statistics

### 🎥 Awareness Videos

* Add awareness video URLs
* Save video links locally
* Play videos inside the application

### ❤️ Health Guidance

* Educational health resources
* Wellness awareness links
* Health improvement recommendations

### 📱 Awareness Reels

* Quick-access awareness reels
* External social media awareness content

### 💡 Suggestions System

* Submit community suggestions
* View previous suggestions
* Mark suggestions as actioned
* Add action notes for follow-up activities

This project currently uses **SharedPreferences** for local storage.

Stored Data:

* Survey submissions
* Awareness video URLs
* Community suggestions

## ▶️ Installation

### Clone Repository


